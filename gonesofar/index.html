Gone So Far

An easy way to keep track of how far you've been walking!


  • Quick and easy to add new goals or to edit and delete old ones!

  • Compare how far you've gone to distances around the world!

  • Share progress on your goals easily with friends and social media!

  • Support for both Miles and Kilometers. Easy and fast to switch!

  • Easily switch between looking at how far you've gone today, during the week, the month, the year, and the entire history of what your phone has recorded!
