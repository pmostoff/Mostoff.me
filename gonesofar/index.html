Gone So Far

A simple and intuitive fitness tracker designed to motivate!


  • Set a Daily Goal to keep you motivated!

  • Get an overview of your movement in the Summary tab!

  • Set Stretch Goals in the Goals tab and compare your progress ot distances that you choose!

  • Apple Watch support with a simple app that let's you view your daily goal progress and complications for at a glance updates!

  • Today Widget support allows you to quickly check your daily goal progress without entering the app!

  • Quickly and easily share your progress on your daily goal or any of your stretch goals through a text or social network!

  • Simple settings that get synced to the Watch app and Today Widget. Set it inone place and the app takes care of the rest!
