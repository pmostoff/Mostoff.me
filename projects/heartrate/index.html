HeartRate

Get a quick overview of your daily heart rate activity!


  • Quickly view your latest heart rate in the Summary tab!

  • Get an easy overview of your minimum, maximum, and average heart rate for the day!

  • View your minimum and maximum heart rate for the day, week, month, or year in the History tab!

  • Apple Watch support let's you view your day’s heart rate statistics at a glance!

  • Today Widget support let’s you quickly check your day’s heart rate statistics without entering the app!
